About Us

We provide trusted and compliant radiation safety solutions to help institutions reduce exposure risks. Our approach combines advanced technology with expert execution to ensure safe, efficient, and sustainable outcomes.

Operating under AERB regulatory standards, we maintain the highest levels of safety and compliance. As an ISO 9001:2015 certified and AERB authorized organization, we specialize in radiation research facilities, industrial radiography, and radionuclide disposal services. Our goal is to deliver reliable solutions while promoting a safer and more responsible environment.
